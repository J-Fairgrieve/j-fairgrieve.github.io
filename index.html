Experience

Shortly after graduating from University, I joined Staples (office supply company) as their Enterprise Bid & Pricing Analyst. In this role, I was responsible for creating pricing models for various bids that would come through the business. My speciality was with the larger bids that stretched across Europe, meaning that I would work closely with colleagues across the European offices. The size and scope of each bid meant that I would need to adapt the pricing accordingly and understand the needs for each project.

After a year working at Staples, I joined Marston's (pub and beer company) as their HR Systems Advisor. This was my first role working with HR data. At Marston's I was responsible for producing regular reports to cover business needs. Some key metrics reported on included turnover, stability and sickness/absence. I was also responsible for collating, presenting and writing reports on the Gender Pay Gap. This was the first major project I was able to take ownership of from start to finish and I thoroughly enjoyed having the freedom to produce write-up reports explaining the changes each year.

Currently I work as an HR Information Systems Analyst at Eversheds Sutherland (law firm).

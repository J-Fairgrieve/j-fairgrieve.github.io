About

Welcome to my portfolio page! My name is James and I am a keen analyst with proven experience working with data, information and analytics in large, diverse and ever-changing businesses. I am primarily skilled in:

  • Microsoft Excel
  • Python
  • SQL
  • Data Visualisation (PowerBI and Tableau)

This portfolio is an extension of my CV, built to showcase some of the projects I have worked on in my free time to develop my skills further. The projects found throughout this portfolio highlight my proficiency with a wide variety of datasets in different scenarios. In my day-to-day job I primarily work with HR data, but I am always keen to expand my knowledge with other data sources.

As an HR data specialist, I have been involved in vital projects, including attrition/turnover and employee stability analysis, employee engagement/sentiment analysis, gender pay gap reporting and diversity/inclusion reporting. Throughout my career, I have demonstrated the ability to extract and analyse data effectively and precisely, reporting the data in engaging ways through various methods; from dashboards to written reports and presentations.

If you have any questions regarding me, my projects or anything else, please find my contact details at the top of this page.
